The Story Behind Ripcord: Navigating Challenges with Determination

Ripcord Consulting is named after the trail at Sugarbush Mountain in Vermont called Sigi's Ripcord, known for its double black diamond challenge along the lift line. Like standing at the top of that steep slope, running a business requires choosing your path amidst uncertainty, navigating unexpected terrain changes, and making swift, strategic decisions while under scrutiny. Just as skiers adapt their lines and overcome obstacles to reach the bottom stronger, so do businesses facing unforeseen challenges who ultimately find their way to success. Ripcord embodies resilience, adaptability, and the determination to reach goals despite the journey’s difficulty.

Feeling stuck? Let’s tackle the mountain together, figure out what’s next and get your business set up for the next phase!
